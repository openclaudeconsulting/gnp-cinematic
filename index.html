How We Build

01 — CONSULTATION

We start with you.

We walk your site and lock the plan: a 40×60 with six posts a side, five bays of clear-span steel. Straight answers on size, layout, and price.

02 — FABRICATION

Cut, welded, built to spec.

Every steel truss is fabricated in-house — cut, jigged, and welded square. Quality is controlled before a single piece leaves the shop.

03 — DELIVERY

Loaded and on the way.

Trusses, posts, and metal are bundled and hauled to your site on our flatbed — the whole package, delivered ready to raise.

04 — INSTALLATION

Raised. Roofed. Done.

Our crew sets the posts, lifts the trusses, and caps it with a painted black metal roof — square, fast, and right the first time.
